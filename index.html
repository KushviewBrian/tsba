Welcome to Excellence

Welcome to The Tobacco Stop & Big Ash Cigar Lounge — Fort Wayne's premier destination for premium cigars, tobacco products, and smoking accessories. Located at 9111 Lima Rd, we feature a spacious walk-in humidor and Indiana's most comfortable cigar lounge.

Our commitment to quality and exceptional customer service has made us the go-to destination for cigar enthusiasts throughout Fort Wayne and beyond. From novice smokers to seasoned aficionados, we provide an unparalleled experience in luxury and comfort.

Premium Cigars & Products

Discover our extensive collection of world-class cigars, tobacco products, and smoking accessories from legendary brands.

Premium Cigars

Our walk-in humidor features legendary brands including Perdomo, Arturo Fuente, Rocky Patel, Drew Estate, Oliva, Romeo y Julieta, Cohiba, and Montecristo. Each cigar is stored in perfect conditions to ensure optimal flavor and quality.

Black Label Trading Co.

We proudly stock James Brown's Black Label Trading Co. and BLK WKS Studio boutique lines. Experience limited releases like Killer Bee, Lawless, NBK, Salvation, and Rorschach - American cigars pushing creative boundaries.

Disposable Vapes

Complete selection of popular disposable vapes including Puff Bar, Hyde, Elf Bar, Lost Mary, Fume, Breeze, Air Bar, and RAZZ Slim Den. Wide variety of flavors and nicotine strengths.

Premium Cigarettes

Full selection including Marlboro, Camel, Newport, American Spirit, Parliament, Kool, Pall Mall, and specialty varieties. Available in regular, light, menthol, and unique blends.

Glass & Accessories

Premium smoking accessories featuring Hemper glassware, water pipes, hookahs, rolling papers, high-quality lighters, cigar cutters, humidors, and elegant ashtrays for the discerning enthusiast.

Specialty Items

Unique selections including Mythical Candle Co. specialty candles with masculine scents perfect for cigar rooms, plus our exclusive Magical Mystery Drawer with rare finds and discounted singles.
