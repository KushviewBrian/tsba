About Us

Welcome to The Tobacco Stop & Big Ash Cigar Lounge — Fort Wayne’s one-stop shop for premium cigars, tobacco products, and smoking accessories. Conveniently located at 9111 Lima Rd, we feature a spacious walk-in humidor and a comfortable cigar lounge where you can unwind with friends.

Products & Brands

Premium Cigars

Explore world-class favorites like Perdomo, Arturo Fuente, Rocky Patel, AJ Fernandez, Drew Estate, Oliva, Romeo y Julieta, Cohiba, Macanudo, Alec Bradley, Gurkha, and Montecristo. Boutique selections include La Aroma de Cuba Mi Amor, Insidious by Asylum, and Blackened M81.

Black Label Trading Co. Feature

We proudly showcase Black Label Trading Co. and BLK WKS Studio lines: Killer Bee, Lawless, NBK, Salvation, Rorschach, Coffin Nails, Heretic, Santa Muerte, Right Hand, and Left Hand.

Small Cigars & Tins

Quick smokes from Cohiba and Drew Estate (ACID, Tabak, Java), plus CAO tins and classics like White Owl, Swisher, Backwoods, and Dutch.

Pipe Tobacco & Pouches

Roll-your-own and pipe favorites: American Club, OHM, and 4 Aces, alongside classic blends.

Glass & Accessories

Premium glassware from Hemper, water pipes, hookahs, rolling papers, lighters, cutters, humidors, and ashtrays.

Disposables & Vapes

Popular options including RAZZ Slim Den disposables and a variety of vape products.

Lifestyle & Gifts

Discover Mythical Candle Co. specialty candles and the exclusive Magical Mystery Drawer — discounted singles and rare finds.

Lounge Experience

Step inside Big Ash Cigar Lounge, Fort Wayne’s most comfortable cigar retreat. Enjoy plush seating, flat‑screen TVs, a private game room, and member lockers for your personal collection.
